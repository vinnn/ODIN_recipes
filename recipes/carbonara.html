Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish that is generally enjoyed by the whole family. Very simple and quick to cook.

Ingredients

  • 100g of lardon
  • Spaghetti or Tagliatelle pasta
  • Fresh cream

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the lardons
  3. Add the lardon and fresh cream to the pasta in a pan
  4. Cook until ready
