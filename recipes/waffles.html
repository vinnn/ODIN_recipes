Waffles

Description

Waffles can be served for dessert or as a snack. Very simple and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 300g of Flour
  • 75g of Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Baking powder (10g)
  • 100g of Butter
  • 0.5L of milk

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, eggs and butter
  2. Add the milk
  3. Add the baking powder
  4. Pour into the waffles maker
